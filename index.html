My Favorite Movies

My Top Three

Spirited Away

Favourite anime

★★★★★

"Spirited Away" is a mesmerizing animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It follows the journey of Chihiro, a young girl trapped in a mysterious and magical world. The film is renowned for its stunning visuals, imaginative storytelling, and profound themes.

Holy Mountain

Artistic masterpiece

★★★★★

"Holy Mountain" is a surreal and visually striking film directed by Alejandro Jodorowsky. It is celebrated for its artistic and symbolic narrative that explores spirituality, alchemy, and the human condition. The film's imagery and allegorical elements make it a unique and thought-provoking cinematic experience.

Persona

Cinematic brilliance by Bergman

★★★★★

"Persona" is a cinematic masterpiece directed by Ingmar Bergman. Known for its deep psychological exploration and innovative storytelling, the film delves into the complexities of identity and human relationships. With exceptional performances and striking visuals, "Persona" is considered a landmark in world cinema.
